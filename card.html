Raccoon

Raccoons have terrible eyesight and are color blind, but have amazing hearing an amazing sense of smell.

  • Scientific Name: Procyon lotor
  • Average Length: 16-28inches
  • Average Lifespan: 3 years
  • Habitat: Heavily Wooded Areas

Immediately indentifiable by thier black "Masks", Raccoons are one of the coolest animals on earth. They can be found everywhere in North America. Raccoons are omnivorous which means they eat anything from plants to small animals. Although their average lifespan is about 2-3 years in the wild, they can live up to 16 years in captivity.
